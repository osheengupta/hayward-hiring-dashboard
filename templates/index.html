About the Hiring Percentage Slider

What does this slider do?

This slider lets you adjust the percentage of overtime hours that will be replaced by hiring new full-time employees.

How to use it:

  • 0% - No new hires, continue with 100% overtime
  • 50% - Replace half of overtime with new hires, keep 50% as overtime
  • 100% - Replace all overtime with new full-time employees

What happens when you adjust it?

The dashboard automatically calculates:

  • How many new employees are needed
  • The cost of hiring those employees (including pension)
  • The remaining overtime costs
  • The total savings or additional costs
